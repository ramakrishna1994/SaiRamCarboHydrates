Description 5-Bromo-4-chloro-3-indolyl b-D-galactopyranoside (X-Gal)
is a histochemical substrate for β-galactosidase.
X-Gal is cleaved by β-galactosidase to yield an insoluble blue preceipitate.
X-Gal is used to detect DNA insertions into the lacZ region of plasmid DNA,
which causes the loss of β-galactosidase activity.
